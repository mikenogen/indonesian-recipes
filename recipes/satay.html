Indonesian Satay

Description

Satay, or sate in Indonesian and Malay spelling, is a Southeast Asian dish of seasoned, skewered and grilled meat, served with a sauce. In this recipe, we're going to make our own homemade Satay Hope readers from all over the world can enjoy it

Ingredients

  1. 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  2. 3 tablespoons tomato sauce
  3. 1 tablespoon peanut oil
  4. 2 cloves garlic, peeled and minced
  5. 1 pinch ground black pepper
  6. 1 pinch ground cumin
  7. 6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cubed
  8. 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  9. 1/4 cup minced onion
  10. 1 clove garlic, peeled and minced
  11. 1 cup water
  12. 1/2 cup chunky peanut butter
  13. 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  14. 2 tablespoons white sugar
  15. 1 tablespoons lemon juice
  16. skewers
